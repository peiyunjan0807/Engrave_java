Participle
分詞

用途
1.形容詞 →修飾名詞：分詞+名詞
Ex：
The dancing girl excited the audiences. 
Boiled water is safe to drink.

2.當主詞補語：S + be動詞+分詞
Ex：
The airplane is flying.
The town appeared abandoned.

3.當受詞補語：受詞+分詞
Ex：
Amy saw the kid crying in the park.
I found her somewhat recovered.


變化規則

現在分詞
1.一般動詞，動詞字尾加-ing
Ex：do→doing, go→going, say→saying
2.動詞字尾為｢子音+e｣，動詞字尾去e加-ing
Ex：write→writing, move→moving, ride→riding
3.動詞字尾為｢短母音+單子音｣，動詞重複字尾子音加-ing
Ex：run→running, put→putting, swim→swimming

過去分詞
1.一般動詞，動詞原形加-ed
Ex：open→opened, call→called
2.動詞字尾為e，動詞原形加-d
Ex：like→liked, hate→hated
3.動詞字尾為子音加y，動詞原形去y加-ied
Ex：cry→cried, study→studied
4.動詞字尾為短母音加子音，動詞原形重複字尾加-ed
Ex：stop→stopped, plan→planned
5.不規則變化
Ex：picnic→picnicked、be→was/were
