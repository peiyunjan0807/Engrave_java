The tense
時態

簡單式
Point 1
表時間及條件的副詞子句，其動詞要用現在式代替未來式，但主要子句仍用未來式或原形動詞(即祈使句)
Ex：
1.If it rains tomorrow, the meeting will be put off.
2.If the room is dark, turn on the light. (祈使句)

Point 2
過去式：表示過去的習慣、狀態、行為與事實等。
Ex：
1.I met one of my high school classmates yesterday.
2.The boy picked up the ashtray.


進行式
Point 1
現在進行式：強調此時此刻正在發生或進行的動作。
1.am / is / are + Ving
2.常與Look!, Listen! / now, at present, at this moment連用
Ex：
1.Listen! Someone is singing in the next room.
2.The traffic in Taipei is getting much worse now.

Point 2
過去進行式：
1.Was / were + Ving
2.強調過去明確時間(幾點)所發生的動作。
3.強調過去某時發生一個動作時，另一個動作正在進行中。
Ex：
1.We were studying English at ten o’clock last night.
2.Mother was cooking when I got home.

Point 3
未來進行式：
1.Shall / will be + Ving
2.表示未來某一明確時間所發生的動作。
Ex：
1.If you come at six, I shall be waiting for you at the door.


完成式
Point 1
現在完成式：
1.have / has + p.p.…for + 一段時間
2.have / has + p.p.… since + 過去時間
since + S. + V(過去)
Ex：
1.They have known each other for a long time. 
2.I have known her since I was a little boy.

Point 2 
過去完成式：
1.過去兩個動作強調先發生的用had p.p., 後發生的用過去式。
2.had p.p.…by + 過去時間
Ex：
1.I felt better after I had taken an aspirin.
2.Patrick had done his homework before I called him last night.

Point 3
未來完成式：
1.Will / shall have p.p.
2.表示未來某時之前已經做完的事情或已有的經驗。
3.常與  by + 未來時間
by the time + S. + V(現) 連用
Ex：
1.By next Sunday you will have stayed with us for five months.
